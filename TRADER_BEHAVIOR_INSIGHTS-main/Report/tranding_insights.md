# Trader Behavior Insights

## Objective

Analyze the relationship between trader performance and Bitcoin market sentiment.

Datasets used:
1. Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index
2. Hyperliquid historical trader data.

---

## Key Insights

1. Traders use higher leverage during Greed sentiment.
2. Trading activity increases significantly in Greed markets.
3. Fear markets show lower trade volume.
4. High leverage trades are associated with larger losses.
5. Win rate slightly improves during Fear periods.

---

## Strategy Recommendations

1. Reduce leverage during extreme Greed sentiment.
2. Fear markets may provide better entry opportunities.
3. Risk management should control leverage exposure.
4. Market sentiment indicators can help adjust trading strategy.
